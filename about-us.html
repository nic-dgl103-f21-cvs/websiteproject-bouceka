Our Story

Our philosophy for Four Quarters was to create a friendly and family place for the Cumberland community to have fresh breakfast and lunch. Since 2017 we have been offering a mix of classic and contemporary Italian and Greek cuisine prepared to the highest standards. Our concept is to make home-style food with high-quality ingredients from local producers at a reasonable price. Every meal is prepared from scratch with care and fresh ingredients. We never use artificial colors, flavors, or preservatives.
